POST /search-posts

ParameterTypeRequiredDescription
search_keywordsstringYesSearch keywords
sort_bystringNoSort order: Latest or Relevance (default: Relevance)
date_postedstringNoDate filter: Past 24 hours, Past week, Past month
startintegerNoStarting offset (default: 0)
